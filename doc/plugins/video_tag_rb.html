Title: Simple Video tag for Jekyll Author: Brandon Mathis brandonmathis.com Description: Easily output MPEG4 HTML5 video with a flash backup.

Syntax {% video url/to/video [width height] [url/to/poster] %}

Example: {% video site.com/video.mp4 720 480 %}

Output: <video width=‘720’ height=‘480’ preload=‘none’ controls poster=‘’>

<source src='http://site.com/video.mp4' type='video/mp4; codecs=\"avc1.42E01E, mp4a.40.2\"'/>

</video>
